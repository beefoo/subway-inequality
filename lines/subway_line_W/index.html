The Subway Above Ground

Whitehall St

The following piece simulates a full ride on the W line of the NYC subway, where the quantity and power of the instruments at any given moment in the song correspond to the median household income of the neighborhood that you are passing through.

Once you start to play the file, find your station, and listen to the song in the background as you ride the subway. The result is a meditative piece in which the dramatic contrasts of a song echo the dramatic differences of income in the city.
